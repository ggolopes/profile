(Brazilian) Ice Cream factory comercial solution (1999)

This commercial system developed in Delphi in 1999 met the demand of an ice cream factory in northeastern Brazil. The system controlled customers, orders, invoices, receipt of invoice payments, inventory control, distribution logistics, and delivery routes, and control of returnable product containers, among other features. The system was standalone due to the lack of network structure in the facilities. This software replaced another older software with a text-based interface (using clipper language), also developed by me.
